<p>By leveraging a high-availability Application Load Balancer as our primary traffic orchestrator, we ensure seamless request distribution across a fleet of EC2 instances that scale dynamically via an Auto Scaling Group to meet fluctuating demand. This architecture is fortified by a self-healing compute layer where Nginx services are monitored by systemd for instant recovery and reinforced by cron-driven health probes to proactively maintain node integrity. To wrap the ecosystem in total observability, we integrate a CloudWatch telemetry suite that transforms performance metrics into real-time alerts, ensuring the entire stack remains resilient, responsive, and operationally transparent.</P>
